Stories

Six captivating news stories, produced during the summer of 2023, seek to amplify local voices usually left unheard, while seeking to deepen the understanding of global issues affecting Miami residents.

The stories, produced by students of the Universidad de Navarra and the University of Miami shed light on these global issues, told by the people they affect the most.
